about blf»

“The Brij Literature Festival(BLF) is a magnificent national level Literature festival in the birth place of Lord Krishna, embodying a rich heritage and a great history of literature. BLF is a beautiful initiative in the form of a two day celebration to promote and preserve the rich heritage of Brij and the nation. The main purpose is to foster and uphold the great Literary and cultural background and portray immense profoundness of the kind of literature Brij actually holds.
The event in itself is going to be an extravaganza. This initiative promises to feature a variety of presentation workshops and readings by authors from various walks of life. The festival is going to provide a great opportunity to the participants and audience to learn and admire the beauty of literature.”

— Literario
