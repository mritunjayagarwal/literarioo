Apurva Jain

I never knew that becoming a part of the GLA Fraternity will be such a blessing in my life. In the year 2013 I joined this prestigious Institution knowing nothing about my hidden capabilities and strengths.
Well thanks to almighty, I was in good hands!
Having no exposure at all in anything to organizing events was a whole journey in itself. It took me almost a year to imbibe into the challenging atmosphere and enriched culture. Then, I started participating in the events and gradually got a hang of everything. From initially participating to driving a whole event by being the Anchor was a great achievement I would say.
But I believe in dreaming, and dreaming Big. With all the knowledge and expertise I had and with the help and experience of all my seniors, I established "Literario".
Finally with all things said and done, and the challenges that came my way, with the right attitude I turned them into stepping stones and at last it was time to mark the starting of a Revolution: Brij Literature Festival. As, I have been referring BLF as a dream it would not have been successful without the able guidance of Anoop Sir.
Thanks to the experience gained from Literario, because of which I am lauded with more than 6 organizational level awards in Tech Mahindra Ltd. in less than 2 years of my services rendered.
I hope to bring more glory and respect to the institutions I am associated with on my way going forward.
Perseverance, Intellect and Confidence has always been the key.
